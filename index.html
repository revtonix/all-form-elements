12 — Details & Summary (Accordion)
What is the <details> element?
The <details> element creates a disclosure widget — a native HTML accordion — that users can open and close. No JavaScript required. The <summary> inside it acts as the visible heading.
Can I nest multiple details?
Absolutely. You can stack as many <details> elements as needed. They are independent of each other; opening one doesn't close the others unless you handle that with JavaScript.
Is it accessible?
Yes — it has built-in keyboard support and is announced correctly by most screen readers as an expandable region. It's one of the most underrated native HTML elements.
